Annotations (3)

  • definition "Process is in BioTop the generic subsumer of anything that "occurs". Processes can span across time and have temporal parts (i.e. there is no time in which all parts of a process are simultaneously present). A special kind of process is the event, which has no temporal extension. It can be regarded as Process Boundary (BFO). Processes have physical or informational entities as participants." ()
