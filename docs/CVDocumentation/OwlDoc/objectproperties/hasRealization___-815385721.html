Object Property: 'has realization'

http://purl.org/biotop/btl2.owl#hasRealization

Annotations (2)

  • definition "Realization occurs if a disposition, a function, or a role manifests itself as a process. Certain information entities can also have realizations, e.g. plans that are realized in processes. Example: the disposition of a bird to fly (which exists even when it does not fly) is realized by its process of flying. " ()
