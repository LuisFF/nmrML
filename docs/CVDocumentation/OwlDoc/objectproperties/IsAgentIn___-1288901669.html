Annotations (2)

  • definition "agentIn (inverse: hasAgent) relates a participant with a process, with the condition that this participant is causally active in the relevant process. This is the reason why agentIn is subsumed by the relation causedBy. Example: A physician is the agent in a operation process; a solvent is the agent in a solving process. " ()
