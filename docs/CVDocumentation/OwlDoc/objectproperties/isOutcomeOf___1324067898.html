  • definition "outcomeOf (inverse: hasOutcome) relates a participant to a process. This participant either - a) comes into being during the process or - b) undergoes some change during the process, so that it instantiates a different class at the end of the process and it constitutes (one of) the main result(s) of the process Example: a Protein molecule is the outcome of a translation process."
